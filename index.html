FAQs

Some Quick insights into the product

What sort of equipment do I need to get started ?

+

A smartphone is about all you need to get started on vidduka, You may get a bluetooth printer if you want to print recipts, but we do offer digital receipts anyway. There is a web portal linked here if you want to access your data from your laptop or desktop.
What if I don't have internet on my shop ?

+

The vidduka app works perfectly fine without internet. However, because your data is stored in the cloud, you need a connection once every so often to synchronize data to the cloud, cellular data is usually sufficient for this case.
For example you could connect to cellular data at the end of your business day to automatically synchronize your data.
I have a team that runs the shop for me, is this something vidduka can help me manage ?

+

Yes !
Vidduka allows you to add your team as users in the system, set permissions for them and allow them do the selling, inventory management etc. You will have transparency to all activity in your shop and will therefore be able to catch any fishy actions.
My inventory list is too long to add manually, Is there a way to bulk add all of them ?

+

Yes !
We have provided a way to add your inventory via a csv or excell file, A sample csv file is available for download in the products page.
